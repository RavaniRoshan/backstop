# Code Of Conduct

Backstop follows the Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct, version 2.1.

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, caste, color, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and direct.
- Focus criticism on technical work and project outcomes.
- Assume maintainers and contributors are balancing tradeoffs.
- Keep security-sensitive details out of public threads.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Sexualized language or attention.
- Trolling or sustained disruption.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject contributions, or ban participants who violate this code of conduct.
